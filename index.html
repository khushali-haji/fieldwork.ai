Fieldwork.ai

Turning field interviews into mapped insights.

The Problem

Urban designers and journalists collect hours of interviews, photos, and notes in the field, but synthesizing patterns across neighborhoods is manual, messy, and time-consuming. Important community concerns can get buried in transcripts.

The Solution

Fieldwork.ai is a mobile-first tool that records interviews, analyzes geo-tagged audio, photos, and video together, and automatically surfaces recurring themes and pain points by location. It connects what’s said with what’s seen.

How AI Works

  • Transcribes interviews automatically
  • Detects recurring themes and urgency levels
  • Links audio insights to related images and videos
  • Clusters concerns by neighborhood

Impact

Researchers save hours of manual coding and can move faster. Communities gain transparent access to what’s being documented and how issues are being addressed.
