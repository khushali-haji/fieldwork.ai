Fieldwork.ai

Turning community conversations into mapped, actionable insight.

The Problem

Field interviews are essential to understanding neighborhoods, but insights are scattered across transcripts, photos, and notes. Manual synthesis is slow, and recurring patterns often go unnoticed.

The Solution

A mobile fieldwork platform that records interviews, attaches geo-tagged media, and uses AI to surface recurring themes and map concerns by location.

Capture

Record interviews and upload photos and video in real time.

Analyze

AI transcribes audio and detects recurring themes and urgency.

Connect

Link visuals, voices, and geography into one unified system.

Visualize

Map patterns across neighborhoods to support better decisions.

Impact

For Researchers: Faster synthesis, stronger reporting, reduced manual coding.

For Community Boards & Agencies: Greater transparency, clearer patterns, better-informed planning.
